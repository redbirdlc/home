My Bookmark Mosaic

How to Customize Your Mosaic:

To add your own icons:

1. Replace the src attribute in each <img> tag with the path to your image

2. Update the onclick function with your desired URL: navigateToSite('https://yoursite.com')

3. Change the alt text to describe your icon

To add more icons:

Copy and paste this template into the mosaic-grid div:

<div class="icon-item" onclick="navigateToSite('YOUR_URL')">
  <img src="YOUR_IMAGE_PATH" alt="YOUR_DESCRIPTION">
</div>

Image recommendations: Use square images (1:1 aspect ratio) for best results. PNG or SVG formats work great for logos.
